Metal Bench Design Comparison

SVG Technical Drawings and Interactive 3D Visualizations

Comparing Visualization Methods

This comparison showcases two complementary approaches to communicating design intent for sheet metal fabrication. Both methods serve different purposes in the design workflow from concept to fabrication.

SVG Technical Drawings

  • Precise dimensions with tolerances
  • Orthographic projections
  • Flat patterns for DXF export
  • Production-ready documentation
  • Print-friendly for shop floor

Interactive 3D Models

  • Intuitive spatial understanding
  • Rotate, pan, zoom controls
  • Assembly and exploded views
  • Client presentation ready
  • Design review and validation
Design Workflow Integration

Use 3D for concept validation and client approval, then SVG flat patterns for SendCutSend fabrication. Both formats complement each other throughout the design-to-manufacturing process.
